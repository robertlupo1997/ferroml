## Usage Notes

1. **Business Line Identification**: Account numbers in the 5xxxx range indicate Cost of Sales, with the first digit after 5 identifying the business line (0=Core, 1=Emerging, 2=Growth)

2. **Payroll Categorization**: Payroll accounts are split by role (Producer vs Support) and by type (Expense, Taxes, Benefits, Bonus)

3. **General Ledger Roll-Ups**: Accounts 60300-60303 represent consolidated payroll categories that roll up detailed accounts

4. **Deal Costs vs COS**: Deal costs (x000-x002) are direct costs associated with specific deals, while COS accounts (x004-x017) are indirect costs allocated to business lines
